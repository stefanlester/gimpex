About Our Corporation

GIMPEX Limited is a company based in Ghana in the western north region , we produce and distribute lumber and round logs products .Primarily lumber used in Construction and lumber industry with a growing emphasis on sustainable forestry operations .

Mission Statement: To sustainably source, process, and export high-quality lumber while ensuring environmental responsibility, customer satisfaction, and long-term partnerships worldwide

Vision Statement:

To be the leading global provider of sustainable and premium-quality lumber, fostering innovation, responsible forestry, and a greener future for generations to come.
