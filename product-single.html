Kiln-Dried Lumber & Custom Dimensions

We provide precisely milled lumber to meet your specific needs. Whether you need standard sizes or custom dimensions, we cater to both small-scale projects and large industrial applications.

Available Lumber Dimensions:

  • Thickness: 1/2" (12mm), 3/4" (19mm), 1" (25mm), 2" (50mm), 3" (75mm), and custom sizes.
  • Widths: 2" (50mm), 4" (100mm), 6" (150mm), 8" (200mm), 10" (250mm), or wider as required.
  • Lengths: 6 feet (1.8m) to 20 feet (6m), available in different cuts.

We also offer custom-cut beams, planks, and boards based on your specifications. Let us know your project needs, and we will supply high-quality lumber tailored to your requirements.
